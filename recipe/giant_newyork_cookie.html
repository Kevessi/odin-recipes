Giant New York Cookie Recipe

Description

The Giant New York-Style Cookie is an indulgent treat that's crispy on the edges, yet chewy and gooey in the middle. Bursting with chocolate chips, this cookie is a true homage to the classic American dessert.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, mix the melted butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar until well combined.
  4. Add the egg and vanilla extract to the sugar mixture and mix well.
  5. Fold in the dry ingredients just until combined.
  6. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  7. Place a large dollop of cookie dough on a lined baking sheet, shaping it into a large, round cookie.
  8. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the edges are golden but the center is still soft.
  9. Let the cookie cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring it to a wire rack to cool completely.

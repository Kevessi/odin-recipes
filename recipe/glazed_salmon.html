Glazed Salmon

Description

This Glazed Salmon recipe is a perfect blend of sweet and savory, featuring a delicious glaze made from a mix of honey, soy sauce, and garlic. Tender, flaky, and full of flavor, this dish can be a weeknight staple or a dinner party showstopper.

ingredients

  • 4 (6-ounce) salmon fillets
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: 1 tablespoon sesame seeds for garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together honey, soy sauce, minced garlic, and grated ginger.
  3. Place salmon fillets on a lined baking sheet, skin-side down.
  4. Brush the fillets with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Pour the glaze over the salmon, making sure to coat the fillets evenly.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until salmon is cooked through and flakes easily.
  7. If desired, sprinkle sesame seeds over the top for garnish.
  8. Let the salmon rest for a few minutes before serving.
